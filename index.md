# Performance Management Training

Welcome to the Performance Management Training program for state government employees. This interactive training will help you develop the knowledge and skills needed to effectively manage and improve program performance.

## Purpose

Performance management is essential for ensuring that government programs achieve their intended outcomes and deliver value to the public. Through this training, you will learn to:

- Define clear program goals and measurable outcomes
- Develop logic models that connect activities to results
- Collect and analyze performance data
- Use evidence to drive program improvements
- Communicate performance results to stakeholders

## Who Should Take This Training

This training is designed for:

- Program managers and supervisors
- Policy analysts
- Budget and planning staff
- Evaluation specialists
- Anyone involved in designing, implementing, or assessing government programs
